Refund Policy

Credits are prepaid and used for API usage. Production checkout remains pending payment-provider approval.

Prepaid credits

Credits are consumed when API requests are processed. Consumed credits are generally non-refundable because upstream model costs are incurred.

Refund window

Unused paid credits may be eligible for refund within 7 days of purchase, subject to fraud checks, payment processor rules, and applicable fees.

Non-refundable cases

Used credits, accounts suspended for abuse or fraud, promotional credits, test credits, and chargebacks initiated outside the stated process may be non-refundable.

Service issues

If a confirmed platform-side issue causes failed charges or unusable service, we may issue credits or refunds at our discretion. Requests: support@modelbridgeapi.com
