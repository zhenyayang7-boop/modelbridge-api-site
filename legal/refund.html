1. Prepaid Credits

Credits are prepaid and used for API usage. Consumed credits are generally non-refundable because upstream model costs are incurred when requests are processed.

2. Refund Window

Unused credits may be eligible for refund within 7 days of purchase, subject to fraud checks, payment processor rules, and any applicable fees.

3. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Credits already used for API requests.
  • Accounts suspended for abuse, fraud, or policy violations.
  • Promotional, free, bonus, or test credits.
  • Chargebacks or disputes initiated outside the stated process.

4. Service Issues

If a confirmed platform-side issue causes failed charges or unusable service, we may issue credits or refunds at our discretion.
